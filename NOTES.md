
# MSGEQ7 Project

## Requirements

- Audio input
  - Microphone input (3.5mm [1/8"] stereo jacks)
    - CUI SJ-3523-SMT (without switch)

- Connects to Smart Sensor Platform
  - 2 DIOs connect to STROBE and RESET and also provides 3.3V power
    - DIO power jumper
  - 1 AIN connects to DCOUT
- 3.3 V operation
  - 100 uF bulk cap
- MSGEQ7 PTH variant (from SparkFun)
  - Socketed

- Application on the mart Sensor Platform provides:
  - Bling output

- Sensor Platform
  - Battery powered
